About Me

Yashvita Mehta is a BA Hons student specialising in Psychology and Business studies at Woxsen University. From her position, as the secretary of Diplomats club, a prestigious student organization that fosters international connections, she has developed her skills in communication, event management and networking, while also had the honour to introduce guests like Mr Yves Letterme, the former prime minister of Belgium to the university. She has gained valuable skills and insights from her internship at Prerna Clinic, a psychiatry clinic in Aurangabad, where she assisted Dr. Ashish Mohide in patient interaction, clinical observation, and record keeping.
She is a dedicated, inquisitive, and enthusiastic individual who welcomes opportunities to expand her horizons and learn something new. She approaches her educational and personal endeavors with diligence and excitement, and aims to make a positive impact in the world through her work.
